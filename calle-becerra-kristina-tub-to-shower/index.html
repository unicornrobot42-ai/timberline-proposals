Overview
Your Bathroom, Transformed

Kristina, we're converting your existing tub setup into a clean, functional walk-in shower that's built to last. Timberline will handle everything from demo through final finish — demo of the existing tub, tile wainscot, and flooring; hot mop waterproofing; full tile labor including a built-in niche; euro slider glass; all plumbing; and all finish work. What's Included
Scope of Work
  • Demo — existing tub, shower tile, tile wainscot, and bathroom flooring removed and hauled off
  • Toilet remove & reset — pulled for demo, properly reset and resealed at completion
  • Hot mop shower pan — waterproof liner installed before tile, code-compliant
  • Walk-in shower construction — 34" × 60" × 96", cement board substrate, ready for tile
  • Tile labor — full shower walls, floor, and built-in niche (tile supplied by client)
  • Built-in shower niche — recessed storage shelf, tiled to match
  • All plumbing — tub-to-shower conversion, valve rough-in, toilet reset, drain connections
  • Euro slider glass enclosure — standard frameless sliding door, 34" × 60" opening
  • Drywall skim coat & paint — bathroom walls patched, skimmed, and painted
  • Vent fan replacement — existing fan removed, new fan installed (material included)
  • Door casing install — labor only; client supplies casing material
  • Baseboard install — labor only; client supplies baseboard material
  • Full cleanup & haul-off — all demo debris removed, site left clean daily
