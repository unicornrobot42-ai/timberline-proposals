The Work

Scope of Work

01

Pre-Construction & Planning

Before we pick up a hammer, we're going to align with you on every detail. We'll finalize your design selections, get your permits pulled with the city and HOA, and make sure everyone's on the same page. This phase is critical — it's where we prevent surprises and set the project up for success. We'll handle all the coordination so you don't have to.

Note: City permit fees and HOA fees are separate and billed directly to you. We typically see pre-construction take 3–6 weeks depending on city review timelines.
02

Whole House Demo & General

Time to clear the slate. We'll demo everything that needs to go — old cabinetry, flooring, fixtures — and haul it all away cleanly. Then we'll install new LVP flooring throughout your home. It's durable, easy to maintain, and looks modern. We'll also remove your old baseboards and install new ones, plus take out that popcorn ceiling and smooth it out. Finally, we're painting the whole house in your selected color and installing recessed lighting in the living room, kitchen, and hallway.

03

Kitchen

This is where the magic happens. We're completely gutting your kitchen and rebuilding it modern. New white shaker cabinets throughout — crisp, clean, timeless. Quartz countertops that'll never need sealing. A beautiful tile backsplash behind the stove. We'll handle all the plumbing, add angle stops where you need them, relocate the fridge water line, and hook up all your appliances. Plus new electrical to power everything safely.

04

Bathrooms (Both)

Both bathrooms get a full gut renovation. New fiberglass shower pans, decorative wall panels, new valves and trim, shower glass enclosures, and all new fixtures. We'll repair any drywall damage and install fresh surfaces throughout. Both get new tile work (your selections) and fresh paint.

+ Optional Add-On

Kitchen Wall Removal (Island Opening): $3,500

Opening up the wall between your kitchen and dining area to create a connected, open-concept space. Includes structural engineering review and temporary support during demo. This is a game-changer if you want more flow between rooms.
