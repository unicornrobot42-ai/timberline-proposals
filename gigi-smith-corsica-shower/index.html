Our Commitment
Warranty & Terms

Our Guarantee: Timberline Build Co stands behind every project. All labor is warranted for one full year from completion date. If something we installed fails or wasn't done right, we come back and fix it — no questions, no runaround. Materials and fixtures carry their full manufacturer warranties, which we'll provide documentation for at project close.


Change Orders: Scope changes happen — we get it. But they require a written change order signed by both parties before work begins. This protects you from unexpected charges and protects us from scope creep. Verbal requests don't count.


Unforeseen Conditions: Sometimes walls hide surprises — water damage, outdated plumbing, code issues. If we uncover anything that affects the scope, we stop immediately, document it with photos, and present options before proceeding. You'll never be blindsided by a bill for work you didn't approve.


Lien Waiver: Upon receipt of final payment, we provide a full unconditional lien waiver. Your home is clear, and our relationship closes clean.
