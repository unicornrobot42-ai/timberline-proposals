Project Overview

Timberline Build Co will design and fabricate two custom built-in closet systems in our shop, then install and finish them on-site at 245 W. Canada St. Both units are built from prefinished imported birch with Ogee-profile MDF doors to match the existing interior millwork — delivering a true built-in look with the craftsmanship to match.

Scope of Work

What's Included

  • Custom fabrication of two (2) built-in pantry-style closet units from prefinished imported birch — built in our shop
  • MDF Ogee-style doors on both units, matching existing interior door profiles
  • 6 shelves total, distributed to your preference across both units
  • On-site installation — units secured and fitted to each space
  • Electrical rough-in and outlet installed inside hallway cabinet
  • On-site paint finish — client selects color
  • All labor, materials, and site cleanup

Not Included

  • Hardware / pulls (client-supplied)
  • Drywall repair beyond cabinet footprint
  • Flooring modifications
  • Lighting inside master bedroom unit
  • Permit (not typically required for cabinet work)
