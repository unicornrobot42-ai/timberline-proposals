Project Overview

This proposal covers full water damage restoration and interior remodel for your Diamond Bar property. The scope includes a complete kitchen refresh, full updates to both bathrooms, new flooring throughout, fresh paint, and all necessary drywall repairs. We'll deliver a clean, updated space that's ready for move-in.
