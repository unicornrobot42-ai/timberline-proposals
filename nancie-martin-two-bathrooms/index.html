Project Overview

Two Complete Bathroom Transformations

We're doing full remodels of both the master bathroom and guest bathroom at 2717 Via Bandita. Both bathrooms include new walk-in showers with frameless glass, new vanities, and heated floors (optional).

The scope includes toilet and shower relocation in both bathrooms — breaking foundation, trenching, and moving drain/supply lines up to 5 feet. A pocket door will be installed in the guest bathroom.

Because you're doing both bathrooms together, we're offering a 10% bundle discount — saving you over $4,300 off the individual pricing.

Customer provides all finishes: tile, grout, vanity, sinks, faucets, toilet, towel bars, TP holder, and mirrors. This estimate is valid for 30 days.
