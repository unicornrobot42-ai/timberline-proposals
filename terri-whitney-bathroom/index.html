Project Overview

Full Bathroom Transformation

We're doing a complete gut-and-rebuild of the bathroom at 25351 Yacht Drive — full demo, new tub/shower tile surround, 60" wainscot tile throughout, vanity install, heated floors, a new exterior window, and updated electrical. A fresh start from the studs out.

All plumbing rough-in and fixture setting is handled by the homeowner. All tile, finishes, and vanity materials are owner-supplied. We handle the skilled labor, framing, and coordination.

This estimate is valid for 30 days from the date above. Material costs subject to change.
