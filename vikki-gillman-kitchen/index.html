Project Overview

This proposal covers a complete kitchen transformation at your Laguna Woods home — including demolition, new cabinet installation, quartz countertops, vertical tile backsplash, LVP flooring, updated electrical and plumbing, and fresh paint throughout. Timberline Build Co will manage all HOA coordination, permitting, and inspections to ensure a smooth, compliant renovation from start to finish.

🔨

Scope of Work

🏗️

Demolition

  • Remove all existing cabinets, cabinet wall, sink, and faucet
  • Remove existing fluorescent lighting panel and prepare for recessed lights
  • HOA will coordinate removal of all existing appliances
  • Haul and dispose of all demolition debris
⚡

Electrical (Labor Only — Fixtures Owner-Supplied)

  • Install three (3) 4" IC-rated recessed LED wafer lights in kitchen soffit Centered within soffit, evenly spaced, first/last ~12–18" from ends, center fixture equally spaced; on dedicated dimmer switch
  • Install three (3) 4" IC-rated recessed LED wafer lights over 120" kitchen peninsula Centerlines at 24", 60", 96" from TV/stove wall; centered over peninsula depth; on dedicated dimmable smart switch
  • Replace existing recessed sink light with new 4" wafer light to match (no relocation or rewiring)
  • Install three (3) separate dedicated dimmer switches — one per lighting group: (1) soffit, (2) peninsula, (3) sink
  • Cap and terminate electrical at former built-in oven location
  • Relocate refrigerator power circuit to sink wall near entry door
🔧

Plumbing

  • Run new refrigerator water supply line to new refrigerator location (no icemaker appliance connection)
  • Connect dishwasher water supply and drain (owner-supplied dishwasher)
  • Install and connect owner-supplied 27" Ruvati undermount stainless steel kitchen sink
  • Install and connect owner-supplied kitchen faucet
  • Reconnect existing garbage disposal (reuse existing unit)
🧱

Drywall

  • Repair all cosmetic drywall damage from demolition (dents, holes, minor damage — no wall straightening or skim coating)
  • Patch all abandoned openings and penetrations
  • Apply orange peel texture to soffit ceiling areas to match existing kitchen ceilings
  • Prepare all surfaces to paint-ready condition (smooth, sanded, dust-free)
  • Existing asbestos-containing ceilings remain 100% undisturbed — no sanding, scraping, or contact of any kind
🎨

Paint

  • Paint all kitchen walls and repaired soffit areas
  • Color selection by owner prior to start
🪵

Flooring

  • Install owner-supplied Shaw Vista Sand Dollar LVP flooring with owner-supplied underlayment
  • Scope: 102 sq ft kitchen area
  • Minor floor preparation and spot leveling over existing vinyl (feathering, patching, minor leveling only — no structural subfloor work)
  • Install with required expansion gaps concealed by baseboards and toe kicks
  • Remove and replace all floor transitions and thresholds at adjacent flooring (flush transitions where possible)
🚪

Cabinetry

  • Install all owner-supplied Cabinets-to-Go white shaker cabinets per approved cabinet drawings
  • Install all fillers, panels, scribes, and toe kicks as required for finished appearance
  • Install all owner-supplied hardware/pulls
  • Field-verify all plumbing and electrical coordination prior to install
  • Installation only — no layout changes, no cabinet fabrication
💎

Countertops

  • Coordinate with countertop vendor
  • Perform single template of all quartz surfaces after cabinet installation is complete
  • Fabricate and install white Carrara quartz countertops
  • Sink and faucet cutouts included
  • Install and seal undermount sink
  • Seal all quartz seams, transitions, and penetrations Final countertop price is based on current estimate and is subject to adjustment pending material calculations and template measurements
🔲

Tile Backsplash

  • Install owner-supplied 3x12 white matte ceramic tile vertically from counter surface to underside of wall cabinets
  • Owner-supplied light gray grout
  • Contractor to advise owner on quantity needed prior to purchase
  • Window backsplash treatment: to be determined with owner prior to tile installation start
📄

HOA Alteration Package + Permits

  • Prepare and submit full Manor HOA Alteration Package on behalf of owner
  • Pull and manage all required permits
  • Coordinate all required inspections Permit and HOA fee costs shown are estimates only. Actual permit fees will be billed to owner at cost. Timberline Build Co will provide documentation of all permit expenditures.
🧹

Cleanup + Site Management

  • Daily debris removal throughout project duration
  • Protect and maintain asbestos-containing ceilings — zero disturbance
  • Haul all demolition and construction waste off-site
  • Final detail clean and broom-clean handoff condition
